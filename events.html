Upcoming Events

Chinese New Year Celebration

Join us as our young artists from different parts of Asia put on a lively Chinese New Year performance, showcasing traditional dances, festive songs, and a playful dragon dance. Dressed in vibrant red and gold outfits, the performance will be filled with enthusiasm, spreading joy and good fortune. The show will feature rhythmic drumming, symbolic gestures for prosperity, and heartfelt wishes for the new year.

Junior Culinary War

Young aspiring chefs compete in an exciting Junior Culinary Cookoff, showcasing their creativity and skills in the kitchen. Each participant will prepare unique dishes using a set of mystery ingredients, impressing the judges with their flavors and presentation. The event will be filled with energy as our junior chefs chop, stir, and plate their creations under time pressure.

Siva Afi

A mesmerizing traditional Samoan fire dance performance performed by skilled dancers will showcase their agility and bravery. Dressed in traditional attire, they twirl flaming knives with precision, moving in perfect rhythm to the beat of energetic Polynesian drumming. Sparks will fly as the performers execute daring spins, flips, and synchronized movements, symbolizing strength and warrior spirit. The breathtaking display of fire and culture will leave you in awe, celebrating the rich heritage of Samoa.

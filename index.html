About Us

Dimond Center of Performing Arts has been helping individuals and communities thrive in their creative aspects to develop an environment where limits are limitless and possibilities are endless. Founded in the early 1900s by a group of musicians in Anchorage, Alaska. DCAP has become a well known institute where dreams come to grow and flourish.

We house over 1,ooo creative artists and encourage each individual to find what speaks to them.We have a multitude of different artistry including but not limited to:


  • Contemporary Dance
  • Contemporary Art
  • Acrobatics
  • Ballet
  • Classical Music
  • Latin Dance
  • Fashion Show
  • Magic and Illusion
  • Culinary Theatre and much more...!


We showcase all of our amazing talents through weekly Showings where our artists perform,embody and explain their works through delicate body movements,precise colorful strokes on their canvases, and beautiful words utter through an open mic room filled with like-minded individuals. We encourage all to visit and experience a world of ingenuity and originality.

Beetlejuice

Its showtime! Based on Tim Burtons dearly beloved film, this hilarious musical tells the story of Lydia Deetz, a strange and unusual teenager whose whole life changes when she meets a recently deceased couple and a demon with a thing for stripes. With an irreverent book, an astonishing set, and a score that is out of this Netherworld, BEETLEJUICE is “SCREAMINGLY GOOD FUN!” (Variety). And under its uproarious surface (six feet under, to be exact), it is a remarkably touching show about family, love, and making the most of every Day-O!

Hamilton

Hamilton is a Broadway musical by Lin-Manuel Miranda that tells the story of Alexander Hamilton, one of America's Founding Fathers. The play follows his rise from an orphaned immigrant to George Washington's right-hand man, his role in shaping the U.S. financial system, and his downfall due to personal and political conflicts. The story highlights his rivalry with Aaron Burr, which ultimately leads to their fatal duel. The musical blends hip-hop, R&B, and traditional show tunes to modernize history and emphasize themes of ambition, legacy, and democracy.

Swan Lake

Swan Lake is a classical ballet composed by Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky. It tells the story of Prince Siegfried, who falls in love with Odette, a princess cursed to live as a swan by the evil sorcerer Rothbart. The spell can only be broken by true love. However, Rothbart tricks Siegfried into pledging his love to Odile, the sorcerer’s daughter, who resembles Odette. Realizing his mistake, Siegfried rushes back to Odette, but their love cannot break the curse. The ballet’s ending varies, with some versions featuring a tragic conclusion where the lovers perish together, while others offer a more hopeful resolution.
